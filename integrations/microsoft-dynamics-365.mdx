# Microsoft Dynamics 365 Integration Guide

## Overview
Connect Rapida with Microsoft Dynamics 365 to access this integrated CRM and ERP platform for customer relationship management and business processes.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access Microsoft Dynamics 365
- After clicking the Microsoft Dynamics 365 integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to Microsoft's login page
- Sign in with your Microsoft account associated with Dynamics 365

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- Enter your Microsoft 365 account credentials
- You may need to complete multi-factor authentication if enabled

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- Microsoft will display the permissions Rapida is requesting
- Review the CRM data access permissions
- Click "Accept" to authorize the integration

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- You'll be redirected back to Rapida
- A success message will confirm the integration
- Your Dynamics 365 data will now be accessible within Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- Access to customer and business data
- Ability to view and modify CRM records
- Access to business process flows and reports

## Troubleshooting
- Ensure you have admin or system customizer role in Dynamics 365
- Check if your organization allows third-party app connections
- Verify that you've entered the correct tenant information
