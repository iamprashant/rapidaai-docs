# LeadSquared Integration Guide

## Overview
Connect Rapida with LeadSquared to utilize this CRM for sales execution and marketing automation tailored for businesses of all sizes.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access LeadSquared
- After clicking the LeadSquared integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to LeadSquared
- Log in to your LeadSquared account if not already logged in

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- Enter your LeadSquared account credentials
- You'll need to provide your Access Key and Secret Key from your LeadSquared settings

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- LeadSquared will show the permissions Rapida is requesting
- Review the lead management and sales access permissions
- Click "Authorize" to approve the integration

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- You'll be redirected back to Rapida
- A success message will confirm the integration
- Your LeadSquared data will now be accessible within Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- Access to leads and sales activities
- Ability to create and update lead information
- Access to marketing automation workflows

## Troubleshooting
- Ensure you have admin rights in your LeadSquared account
- Check if you've entered the correct API credentials
- Verify that your LeadSquared plan supports API integrations
