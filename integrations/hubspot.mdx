# HubSpot Integration Guide

## Overview
Connect Rapida with HubSpot to leverage this CRM platform for marketing, sales, and customer service with tools for inbound marketing and sales.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access HubSpot
- After clicking the HubSpot integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to HubSpot
- Sign in to your HubSpot account if not already logged in

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- Enter your HubSpot account credentials
- Select the HubSpot portal you want to connect if you have multiple

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- HubSpot will display the permissions Rapida is requesting
- Review the data access permissions for contacts, deals, and marketing tools
- Click "Grant Access" to approve the integration

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- You'll be redirected back to Rapida
- A success message will confirm the integration is complete
- Your HubSpot data will now be accessible within Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- Access to contacts, companies, and deals
- Ability to view and modify marketing campaigns
- Access to sales and customer service tools

## Troubleshooting
- Ensure you have admin access to your HubSpot portal
- Check if your HubSpot subscription level supports API integrations
- Verify that you've granted all necessary permissions
