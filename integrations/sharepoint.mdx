# SharePoint Integration Guide

## Overview
Connect Rapida with SharePoint to access document management, intranet capabilities, and integration with Office 365 collaboration tools.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access SharePoint
- After clicking the SharePoint integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to Microsoft's login page
- Sign in with your Microsoft account that has access to SharePoint

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- Enter your Microsoft 365 account credentials
- You may need to use multi-factor authentication if enabled on your account

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- Microsoft will show a permissions request screen
- Review the permissions Rapida is requesting for SharePoint access
- Click "Accept" to grant the permissions

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- You'll be redirected back to Rapida
- A confirmation message will appear indicating successful integration
- Your SharePoint sites and documents will now be accessible in Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- Access to SharePoint sites, lists, and document libraries
- Ability to read and write documents
- Integration with Office 365 workflows

## Troubleshooting
- Ensure you have sufficient permissions in SharePoint (site owner or admin)
- Check if your organization allows third-party app connections
- Verify that you're using the correct Microsoft account associated with SharePoint
