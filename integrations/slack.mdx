# Slack Integration Guide

## Overview
Connect Rapida with Slack to enhance your collaboration hub that connects people with the information they need.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access Slack
- After clicking the Slack integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to Slack's authorization page
- Log in to your Slack workspace if not already logged in

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- Select the Slack workspace you want to connect with Rapida
- Ensure you have admin privileges for the selected workspace

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- Slack will display the permissions Rapida is requesting
- Review the channel access, messaging capabilities, and user information permissions
- Click "Allow" to authorize the integration

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- You'll be redirected back to Rapida
- A confirmation message will indicate successful connection
- Your Slack workspace will now be integrated with Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- Access to channels and direct messages
- Ability to send messages and notifications
- Access to user information and workspace data

## Troubleshooting
- Ensure you're a workspace admin or have sufficient permissions
- Check if your Slack plan supports third-party integrations
- Verify that you've selected the correct workspace
