# Zoho CRM Integration Guide

## Overview
Connect Rapida with Zoho CRM to leverage this cloud-based CRM with tools for sales, marketing, and customer support.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access Zoho CRM
- After clicking the Zoho CRM integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to Zoho
- Log in to your Zoho account if not already logged in

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- Enter your Zoho CRM account credentials
- You may need to provide your organization ID or domain

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- Zoho will display the permissions Rapida is requesting
- Review the CRM data access permissions
- Click "Accept" to approve the integration

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- You'll be redirected back to Rapida
- A confirmation message will indicate successful connection
- Your Zoho CRM data will now be accessible in Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- Access to contacts, leads, and deals
- Ability to create and update CRM records
- Access to marketing campaigns and analytics

## Troubleshooting
- Ensure you have admin rights in your Zoho CRM account
- Check if your Zoho plan supports API integrations
- Verify that you've enabled API access in your Zoho CRM settings
