## Overview
Connect Rapida with Google Drive to utilize cloud storage with real-time collaboration, sharing capabilities, and document management.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access Google Drive
- After clicking the Google Drive integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to Google's sign-in page
- Sign in with your Google account

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- Enter your Google account credentials
- Complete any two-factor authentication if enabled

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- Google will display the permissions Rapida is requesting
- Review the file access and modification permissions
- Click "Allow" to grant access to your Google Drive

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- You'll be redirected back to Rapida
- A success message will confirm the integration is complete
- Your Google Drive files will now be accessible within Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- View and manage files and folders in your Google Drive
- Create, edit, and delete files
- Access to sharing settings and collaboration features

## Troubleshooting
- Make sure you're signed into the correct Google account
- Check if you have any organizational restrictions on third-party apps
- Ensure you have sufficient storage space in your Google Drive
