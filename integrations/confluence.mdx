# Confluence Integration Guide

## Overview
Connect Rapida with Confluence to enhance your collaborative workspace with templates, integration with Jira, and advanced search capabilities.

## Integration Process

### Step 1: Access Confluence
- After clicking the Confluence integration box in Rapida, you'll be redirected to Confluence
- Log in to your Confluence account if not already logged in

### Step 2: Add Credentials
- In Confluence, you'll be prompted to enter your admin credentials
- Provide your Confluence username and password or API token

### Step 3: Approve Authorization
- Confluence will display an authorization screen showing the permissions Rapida is requesting
- Review the permissions for workspace access, page creation, and content management
- Click "Allow Access" to authorize Rapida

### Step 4: Confirm Connection
- Once approved, you'll be redirected back to Rapida
- Rapida will display a confirmation message indicating successful connection
- Your Confluence workspace will now be accessible within Rapida

## Permissions Granted
- Read and write access to Confluence pages and spaces
- Ability to create, edit, and manage content
- Access to templates and workspace structures

## Troubleshooting
- Ensure you have admin-level permissions in Confluence
- Check that you've entered the correct credentials
- Verify that third-party app integration is enabled in your Confluence settings
