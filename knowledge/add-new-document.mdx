Adding documents to your knowledge base is easy. Follow these steps to enhance your AI's knowledge:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Knowledge Base">
    Navigate to the Knowledge section in your dashboard.

    ![Knowledge Base Overview](/images/knowledge/knowledge_overview.png)

  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Addition Method">
    Click on "Add document manually" to start adding a new document.

    ![Add Document Options](/images/knowledge/add_document_options.png)

  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Document Type">
    You have two main options:
    1. Upload text files (e.g., .txt, .doc, .docx, .pdf)
    2. Structure document (FAQ, Product, Article)

    Choose the option that best fits your document.

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uploading Text Files

If you're uploading a standard text file:

1. Drag and drop your file into the upload area, or click to select from your computer.
2. Supported formats: .txt, .doc, .docx, .pdf (Max 10 MB per file)
3. Click "Upload New Document" to add it to your knowledge base.

![Upload Document](/images/knowledge/upload_document.png)

## Structuring a Document

If you're adding a structured document:

1. Choose the document type:

   - Help / QnA (for FAQs)
   - Product Catalog
   - Blog Article

   ![Select Document Type](/images/knowledge/add_document_options.png)

2. Fill in the required fields for your chosen document type.
3. Upload any associated files if needed.
4. Click "Upload New Document" to add it to your knowledge.

After uploading, your document will be processed and integrated into the knowledge. This may take a few moments depending on the file size and complexity.

**Tip**: Adding diverse, high-quality documents improves your AI's ability to assist users effectively.
