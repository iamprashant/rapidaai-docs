# Replicate

Replicate offers a 7 billion parameter open-source model by Meta fine-tuned for chat purposes, providing a cost-effective option for conversational AI.

## Configuration Steps

1. Navigate to the **Providers and Models** section in your Rapida.ai dashboard
2. Locate and click on the **Replicate** card
3. In the "Create provider credential" popup:
   - The provider will be pre-selected as **Replicate**
   - Enter a **Key Name** (e.g., "Replicate-Prod" or "Replicate-LLaMA")
   - Enter your **API Token** from Replicate
4. Click **Configure** to save your settings

## Obtaining an API Token

1. Sign up or log in to [Replicate](https://replicate.com/)
2. Navigate to your account settings
3. Go to the "API tokens" section
4. Create a new token or copy an existing one

## Supported Models

Replicate hosts thousands of models including:
- LLaMA 2
- Stable Diffusion
- SDXL
- ControlNet
- Many open-source AI models across different domains

## Common Use Cases

- Text generation
- Image generation and editing
- Video generation
- Audio processing
- Research and experimentation with open-source models
