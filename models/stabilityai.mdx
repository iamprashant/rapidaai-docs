# Stability AI

Stability AI is a developer of open AI models for images, language, audio, video, 3D, and biology, offering comprehensive creative tools.

## Configuration Steps

1. Navigate to the **Providers and Models** section in your Rapida.ai dashboard
2. Locate and click on the **StabilityAI** card
3. In the "Create provider credential" popup:
   - The provider will be pre-selected as **StabilityAI**
   - Enter a **Key Name** (e.g., "Stability-SDXL" or "StabilityAI-Prod")
   - Enter your **API Key** from Stability AI
4. Click **Configure** to save your settings

## Obtaining an API Key

1. Sign up or log in to [Stability AI Platform](https://platform.stability.ai/)
2. Navigate to your account settings
3. Go to the "API Keys" section
4. Generate a new API key
5. Copy the key value

## Supported Models

- Stable Diffusion XL
- Stable Diffusion 3
- StableLM (language models)
- StableCode (code generation)
- Stable Audio
- And other multimodal generative models

- Text-to-image creation
- Style transfer

- Creative design assistance
- 3D asset generation
